Special Tile Technologies

Engineered for performance. Designed for people. Our specialty tiles go beyond aesthetics to deliver function, safety, and sustainability.

01
Germ Free Tiles
Anti-bacterial and anti-viral surfaces for kitchen countertops and bathroom walls. Clinically tested formulation inhibits 99.9% of harmful microorganisms.
Anti-Bacterial · Anti-Viral
02
TAC Tiles
Tactile non-slip indicator tiles engineered for visually challenged users. Compliant with international accessibility standards for public spaces.
Accessibility · Non-Slip
03
Anti-Static Tiles
Electrostatic discharge (ESD) control surfaces for data centers, clean rooms, hospitals, and industrial environments sensitive to static electricity.
ESD Control · Industrial
04
Cool Roof Tiles
Heat-reflective ceramic tiles for terraces, balconies, and courtyards. Reduces surface temperature by up to 30°C compared to standard tiles.
Heat Resistant · Solar Reflective
